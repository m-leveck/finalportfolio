Project Overview

The goal of our project was to create a Harm Reduction Kit. This small, portable, kit includes practical and educational resources to reduce the harm of drugs and minimize overdoses. Our kit includes tools like narcan, fentanyl testers, rubbing alcohol, and needle disposal. Within this kit, there is a QR code that connects you to an app which includes further first aid training and educational resrouces.

Social Issue of Concern

This product reduces harm by providing accessible information and destigmatizing drug use. It includes easy to understand resources and prioritizes health over shame.
